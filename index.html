Elevating Elderly Care Standard Together.

We are dedicated to providing compassionate and preofessional care for the elderly.

Who we are

At Everygreen, we believe that every senior deserves to age with dignity, respect, and compassion. Our agency was founded with a mission to provide exceptional care and support to elderly individuals and their families, ensuring they receive the assistance they need to live fulfilling and independent lives.

Why Choose Evergreen

Experience compassionate Caregiving Tailored to Your Loved One's Needs.

Compassionate Caregivers

Our team comprises trained professionals who are not only skilled in elderly care but also compassionate in their approach. They treat your loved ones like family, ensuring their comfort and well-being at all times.

Personalized Care Plans

We understand that every individual has unique needs and preferences. That's why we tailor our care plans to match the specific requirements of each client, providing personalized attention and support.

Experience and Expertise

With years of experience in the field of elderly care, we have the knowledge, skills, and expertise to meet the unique needs and challenges faced by seniors.

Comprehensive Services

From daily assistance with tasks to specialized memory care programs, we offer a wide range of services to cater to diverse needs. Whether it's companionship, medication management, or mobility assistance, we've got you covered.

Family-Centered Approach

We keep open lines of communication, providing regular updates and actively involving family members in decision-making to ensure that our clients receive the best possible care and support.

Reliability and Trustworthiness

When you choose Evergreen, you can trust that your loved ones are in safe hands. We prioritize their safety, comfort, and happiness, providing reliable care that you can count on.

Services

Comprehensive Elderly Care Solutions Tailored to Your Needs.

Companion Care

Providing companionship emotional support, and engagement in meaningful activities to combat and isolation.

Memory Care

Specialized care for seniors with Alzheimer's disease, dementia, or memory impairment, including cognitive stimulation and memory enhancing activities.

Personal Care

Assisting with activities of daily living such as battling, grooming, dressing, and toileting with dignity and respect.

Medication Management

Ensuring that medications are taken on time and in the correct dosage, under the supervision of trained professionals.
